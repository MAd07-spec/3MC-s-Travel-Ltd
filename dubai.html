Burj Khalifa

The Burj Khalifa, standing at 828 metres, is Dubai’s most iconic landmark and the tallest building in the world. Visitors can enjoy panoramic views of the city, desert, and ocean from its observation decks on the 124th, 125th, and 148th floors. Inside, it features luxury lounges, fine dining restaurants, and world-class architecture that reflects Dubai’s ambition and innovation. A visit at sunset or during the evening light show offers an unforgettable experience.

  • Observation decks on the 124th, 125th and 148th floors
  • Get ticket for top floor as cheap as 40 euro
  • Luxury residences and the Armani Hotel
  • Unrivalled 360° city views — best at sunset

Desert Quad Biking

Experience the thrill of desert quad biking in Dubai's stunning desert landscape. Guided tours take you through sand dunes, offering an adrenaline-pumping adventure suitable for both beginners and experienced riders. Explore the vast expanse of the desert, enjoy breathtaking views, and immerse yourself in the unique beauty of the Arabian desert. Many tours also include traditional Bedouin experiences, such as camel rides and desert camping.

  • Guided quad biking tours through sand dunes
  • Suitable for all skill levels
  • Stunning desert landscapes and views
  • Optional Bedouin experiences included

Museum of the Future

The Museum of the Future in Dubai is a visionary space dedicated to exploring the future of science, technology, and innovation. The museum features interactive exhibits, immersive experiences, and cutting-edge technologies that showcase advancements in artificial intelligence, robotics, space exploration, and more. Visitors can engage with futuristic concepts and gain insights into how these innovations may shape our world in the years to come.

  • Interactive exhibits on future technologies
  • Immersive experiences and cutting-edge displays
  • Focus on AI, robotics, space exploration, and more
  • Insights into future innovations

Aquaventure

Aquaventure Waterpark, located at Atlantis The Palm in Dubai, is one of the largest and most exciting waterparks in the Middle East. It features a wide range of thrilling water rides, slides, and attractions for all ages. Visitors can enjoy adrenaline-pumping experiences like the Leap of Faith slide, relax on lazy rivers, or explore the marine habitats filled with exotic sea creatures. The park also offers dining options and cabanas for a comfortable day out.

  • Thrilling water rides and slides
  • Marine habitats with exotic sea creatures
  • Relaxing lazy rivers and wave pools
  • Dining options and private cabanas
